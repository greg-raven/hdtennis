Contact me

I regret to say that due to the high number of requests I’ve received over the years from stupid and sometimes rude time-leeches from around the world, I now accept work from and answer questions for players here in the High Desert only. If you need answers to your tennis questions and you don’t live close by, please contact a USRSA stringer in your area.

If you can’t wait to run into me on court, you can e-mail me, or try to reach me by phone or text at six four six one seven two eight in area code seven six zero.

Please note that calls after 8:00 p.m. go straight to voicemail.

You can also try me on Skype, although I’m not often connected.
