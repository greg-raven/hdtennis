Services Available

For me, being a racquet technician means doing more than cutting out the old strings and throwing in some new strings. In additional to the attention to detail you would expect from an MRT, I also offer some less-obvious services.

  • Racquet stringing and re-stringing
  • Racquet modifications and customization
  • Choosing a new racquet
  • Finding and maintaining older racquets
  • Selecting a new string
  • Prices

I regret to say that due to the high number of requests I've received over the years from stupid and sometimes rude time-leeches from around the world, I now perform this service for players here in the high desert only. If you need answers to your tennis questions and you don’t live close by, please contact a USRSA stringer in your area.
