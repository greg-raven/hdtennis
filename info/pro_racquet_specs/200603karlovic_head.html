Ivo Karlovic’s HEAD Flexpoint Instinct
2006 Pacific Life Open

At the 2006 Pacific Life Open, Ivo Karlovic brought in his “favorite” HEAD Flexpoint Instinct, and six brand new frames, just received from HEAD. Each of these frames was unstrung.

As you can tell, someone at HEAD went to some effort to make the balance point exactly the same on each of the new racquets. Fortunately, there was enough lead tape under the bumperguard to allow the six new frames to be matched to Ivo’s “favorite” frame.
