Swingweights from the 2010 BNP Paribas Open

Here are a few swingweights I managed to grab during the tournament.

Player Racquet Swingweight
Aspelin Wilson 363
Bryan, B. Prince 368
Bryan, M. Prince 354
Davydenko Prince 352
Moya Babolat 399
Odesnik Babolat 385
Soderling Head 392

Note that the Bryan brothers and Davydenko are using racquets that are longer than the retail versions.

All swingweights are for strung racquets.

USRSA has published on-line the stringing logs from this tournament, also thanks to Dave Mindell and Casey Maus at Star Stringing. Stringing logs are available to USRSA members only.
