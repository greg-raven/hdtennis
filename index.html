Welcome

My name is Greg Raven, and I offer professional-quality tennis racquet re-stringing and customization at affordable prices for players in the high desert area, including Apple Valley, Victorville, and Hesperia. I regret to say that due to the high number of requests I’ve received over the years from stupid and sometimes rude time-leeches from around the world, I now accept work from and answer questions for players here in the high desert only. If you need answers to your tennis questions and you don’t live close by, please contact a USRSA stringer in your area.

I try to play in the morning at Doris Davies Park or the courts at the Apple Valley golf course. If you can’t catch up with me at either of those places, give me a call to arrange a meeting or drop-off. If you need your racquet in a hurry, make certain to call ahead of time to ensure that I will be at the courts to receive your racquet.

Official stringer of the 2007 American Housing Group Tennis Championships.
